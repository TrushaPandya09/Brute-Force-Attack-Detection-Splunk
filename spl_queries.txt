SPL Queries:
Step 1: Data Ingestion
Query:  index=botsv3
Step 2 : Authentication Failure Analysis 
Query:
 index=* failed OR failure
| stats count by host source
| sort -count
Step 3 : Top Attacking Systems 
Query:
index=* failed OR failure
| stats count by host 
| sort -count
| head 10
Step 4: Targeted Users
Query:
index=* failed OR failure
| stats count by username
| sort -count
| head 10
Step 5: Brute Force Detection 
Query:
index=* failed OR failure
| stats count by host source
| where count > 5
| sort -count
Step 6 : Brute Force Attack Time Analysis 
Query:
index=* failed OR failure
| stats count min(_time) as first max(_time) as last by host source
| where count > 10
| convert ctime(first) ctime(last)
